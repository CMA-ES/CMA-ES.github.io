Part of cma.sigma_adaptation (View In Hierarchy)

Compares median fitness to the 27%tile fitness of the previous iteration, see Ait ElHara et al, GECCO 2013.

>>> assert es.optimize(cma.ff.elli).result[1] < 1e-9
